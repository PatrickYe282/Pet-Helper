# PRD: Lost Pet Finder

## Who is the site for?
Families — both kids and parents — whose pet has just gone missing and need immediate guidance.

## What problem does it solve?
In the first panicked minutes after a pet goes missing, families don't know what to do first. The site gives them a clear action plan and a fast way to alert neighbors.

## What sections are must-have?
- A clear title and intro
- Recently Reported Lost Pets
- What To Do If Your Pet Is Missing (step-by-step)
- How To Help a Neighbor's Lost Pet
- A final call to action (report a lost pet / share the page)

## What can wait until later?
- A checklist feature
- A short quiz
- Myth vs. fact cards
- Visual polish and stronger layout
- Recently Reported Lost Pets (needs a database — too big for MVP)
